üî¨ Analysis Methodology

Image Processing: ErosionWatch uses computer vision algorithms to detect reference pins or rulers in your photos and calculate the pixel-to-millimeter scale for accurate measurements.

Erosion Measurement: The system measures the exposed length of reference pins to determine soil loss since the pins were originally installed.

Risk Classification: Locations are categorized as Low (<5mm), Medium (5-15mm), or High (>15mm) risk based on measured soil loss.

Recommendations: Planting suggestions focus on proven erosion control methods, particularly contour hedgerows using vetiver grass or similar deep-rooted vegetation.
